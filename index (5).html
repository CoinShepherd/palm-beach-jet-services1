Palm Beach Jet Services serves as an agent in obtaining air charter services for our clients. We select carriers that are certificated by the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) and registered with the U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT). Carriers are solely responsible for the air transportation arranged by Palm Beach Jet Services for its clients. Palm Beach Jet Services does not own or operate the aircraft on which our clients fly. Palm Beach Jet Services is not a direct or indirect air carrier. All flights arranged by Palm Beach Jet Services are operated by properly licensed and certified FAR Part 135 air carriers. Palm Beach Jet Services is an air charter broker and does not operate aircraft. All charter services are provided by independent FAA-certificated Part 135 air carriers that exercise full operational control of each flight.
